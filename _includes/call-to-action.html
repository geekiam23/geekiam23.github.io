About Me

In my experience as a Student Ministry Director, Non-Profit Director, and Operations Manager, I had the opportunity to work with exceptional leaders who taught me the value of being a learner, self-starter, and always leaving a place better than I found it. As I began to explore the world of software development, I drew on the invaluable lessons I learned from my previous leaders and stepped out to forge a new path in my career.

When my wife, Katie, and I relocated to Kansas City last year, I researched development bootcamps and discovered this skill-set opened the door to hundreds of different career opportunities. Not only could I do something that was challenging and fun, but I could also help companies achieve their goals by creating distinctive and intuitive products/websites.

I began searching for a program providing a well-rounded, thorough knowledge base of Software Development and Programming. With the weekly one-on-one mentoring, assignments that work towards building a portfolio, and job placement record, the Bloc bootcamp stood head and shoulders above the rest. I currently work as a developer at Soleran, a tech company in Overland Park, KS, where I apply the knowledge I've gained from Bloc to create application and websites.
